UI solutions

Postcode + Button

  • Super Simple, time efficient
  • Everybody knows how it works
  • Easy to find the requested data from the results

Search as you type

  • Fancy
  • Depending on address, might be time requiring
  • Partial address results are unusable
  • Risky with international data, due to formatting differences
    • Common street names issue. (Bath Road, Highway Avenue, High Street)
    International address order: lookup can fail with incorrect orders. (hungary likes: Town, Postcode, Street)
